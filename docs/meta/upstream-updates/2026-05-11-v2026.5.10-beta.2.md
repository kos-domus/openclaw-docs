# OpenClaw 2026.5.10-beta.2

Small follow-up beta, mostly hardening the beta.1 line instead of changing direction.

## Highlights

- No headline feature pivot: this is mainly a stability pass on the same 2026.5.10 themes.
- The most visible additions are around plugin-aware `gateway status --deep`, ACP backend startup probing, and stricter OpenAI-compatible replay sanitization.

## Breaking or operator-impactful changes

- ACPX startup probing now blocks Gateway "ready" until the backend is actually usable or has failed loudly. Good for truthfulness, but it changes startup timing expectations.
- SIGUSR1/in-process restart now rereads config from disk, which is the right behavior but worth noting for operators who were implicitly relying on stale boot snapshots.

## Fixes most worth caring about

- `gateway status --deep` stays plugin-aware during install/update smoke checks.
- Voice/Ollama, strict OpenAI-compatible providers, and Telegram media-root routing all got practical correctness fixes.
- Beta.2 also tightened stale-profile and stale-config behavior enough to matter for fleets that live on long-running gateways.
